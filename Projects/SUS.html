Project Content

Android Mobile Development (Java, Gradle); User Interface Design


As part of LA Hacks 2017, the annual hackathon in Los Angeles, California, I worked with a team of 4 to prototype an Android application called SUS, used to track and visualize pollution in the Los Angeles area, while also giving end-users the opportunity of reporting pollution, and creating events to address these issues. SUS, a play on words of "SOS" and "Sustainability", was awarded the Most Sustainable Hack of 2017.

Design Process

Initial Brainstorming and User Profiles

For purpose of prototyping our app, we designed the following functions we wanted to showcase in our application:

Event Setup

Users should be able to create events in our app associated with areas requiring environmental protection.

Geographic Data Visualization

Using the LA City API and Google Maps, we wanted to visualize all the pollution and environment issues in the LA Area.

Connecting with Peer Users

Users should also be able to connect and socialize with the community of environmental activists.
