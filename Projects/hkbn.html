Hong Kong Broadband Network

Overview


As a Business Enterprise IT Intern at Hong Kong Broadband Network (HKBN), one of Hong Kong's leading telecommunications company, I worked on a variety of projects ranging from user experience research to an internal web application for visualizing and organizing customer data.


(As my work is under the company's NDA, I am not allowed to describe all my projects in detail. However, all my projects have been stored and used by the company for future software development)

Projects

User Experience Research

At the beginning of my internship, I was responsible for independently acquiring user experience data when potential customers use the company's web services to purchase products and make transactions. To do so, I was required to design and execute a study on my own.

Though I came from a Psychology background, I had no prior experience with user experience research. However, upon conducting simple searches online, and with guidance from my team, I learned more about the nuances between behavioral research and user studies. Hence, I designed my own metrics, and began by performing a case study evaluating the functionality and interface design of the company's web services in contrast to their local and international competitors. Some of the features I investigated included navigating to products; making purchases; and scalability on other mobile devices such as tablets and smartphones.

Extracting common themes from these features, I then further referenced data obtained by the company's marketing team, who was also investigating customer experience with our web services, to compile an internal report that was later presented and shared with the CTO and IT directors of the company. In this meeting, I also offerred some actionable solutions moving forward. Findings and data were kept and referenced by the company.

Internal Customer Data Web Application

In order to better respond and handle customer's online requests and visualize statistics pertaining to customer purchases, our team was tasked to design an internal web application that would be used for further marketing operations.

During this project, I gained first-hand experience in the web development process, such as learning how to write HTML, CSS, and Javascript code. I also designed and prototyped exemplar front-end interfaces for the application, which were used and referenced by the company.
